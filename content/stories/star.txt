== If you are yet another falling star responsible for fulfilling thousand wishes of all those who witness your fire, be it. Be the star that ends with a wave==
== July 7th, 2025 Time And Space==
== room: tenderness ==








What are you other than a merely invisible tiny dot on the space time graph. But you are there. Each of us. Yet the graph extends to infinity on each side. Still, you are a tautology, your existence is recorded. Perhaps, someone is keeping a track of it. Perhaps, the tracker will bring back the favourite characters in next universe. And if not, if you are yet another falling star responsible for fulfilling thousand wishes of all those who witness your fire, be it. Be the star that ends with a wave. The beginning and end of which are two corners of your stretched smile.
